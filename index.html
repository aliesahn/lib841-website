Welcome to my personal website homepage. My name is Allison Fitzmaurice and I am a Teen Library Assistant with the Louisville Free Public Library. I also work at a coffeeshop as a barista.

Exciting News- Oculus Quest 2

Recently the Louisville Free Public Library acquired an Oculus Quest 2 VR Headset for the Highlands-Shelby Park Library. I will be running a Teen VR Headset program every Wednesday starting in July where teen patrons can learn how to use the Oculus Quest 2 and experience fun and educational VR applications.

Apps Available on the HSP Oculus Quest 2

  • Jurassic World
